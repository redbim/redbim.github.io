Services and Pricing

Professional as-built services tailored to your needs

Our Services

We offer comprehensive as-built documentation services to help you plan, design, and execute your renovation projects with confidence. Our services are designed to be fast, accurate, and contractor-friendly.

As-Built Floor Plans

Clean, dimensioned existing-condition plans created from your measurements, photos, or old drawings. Perfect for understanding your space, planning renovations, and getting accurate quotes from contractors.

  • Dimensioned floor plans in PDF and CAD formats
  • Room labels and square footage calculations
  • Door and window locations
  • Wall thicknesses and structural elements

Revit As-Built Model

A structured Revit model of existing conditions for renovation planning and coordination. Ideal for design-build teams and contractors who need to coordinate multiple trades.

  • Fully modeled existing conditions in Revit
  • Organized by floor and room
  • Ready for renovation design work
  • Compatible with all Revit versions

Renovation Layout Concepts

Proposed layout options that help clients make decisions and help contractors quote accurately. Multiple design concepts to explore different possibilities for your space.

  • Multiple layout options for comparison
  • Furniture and fixture placement
  • Traffic flow and space optimization
  • 3D renderings available
