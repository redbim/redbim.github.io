About Red BIM

Professional as-built services for your renovation projects

Who We Are

Red BIM is a small team of BIM professionals who are passionate about creating beautiful and functional spaces. We specialize in turning measurements, photos, and existing drawings into clean floor plans, Revit models, and renovation-ready layouts for homeowners, contractors, and realtors.

Whether you need an accurate as-built, a clean model to coordinate trades, or a clear set of drawings to communicate scope, we deliver organized files that your team can actually use.

Our Mission

We're built for small teams who need professional outputs. Our mission is to provide low-cost, high-value products that get the job done right. We understand that renovation projects require accurate documentation, and we're here to make that process fast, accurate, and contractor-friendly.

What We Do

As-Built Documentation

We create clean, dimensioned existing-condition plans from your measurements, photos, or old drawings. Our floor plans are designed to be contractor-friendly and easy to use for quoting, scoping, and coordination.

Revit Modeling

We build structured Revit models of existing conditions for renovation planning and coordination. Our models are organized, accurate, and ready for your design team to work with.

Layout Concepts

We develop proposed layout options that help clients make decisions and help contractors quote accurately. Our concepts help you understand your space and compare different possibilities.

Client-Focused Service

We work directly with homeowners, contractors, and realtors to deliver exactly what you need. Our team is responsive, professional, and committed to your project's success.
